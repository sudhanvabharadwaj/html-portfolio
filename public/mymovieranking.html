The Best Movies of All Time According to Sudhanva

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Shawshank Redemption

Hands down one of the greatest movies of all time! I'm a huge fan of the story and the performances just made me a bigger fan.

Interstellar

A beautiful story. A visual treat for a science nerd like me and the top notch music made it even more intense.

Kantara

A brilliant story with equal doses of humor, action and thrill. The performances were next level which takes it to the next level.
